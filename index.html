Seven Sisters

Step into a world where the mountains touch the sky, the air tastes of pine, and every trail leads to a new discovery.

"Tucked away in the far reaches of Northeast India, the Seven Sisters— Arunachal Pradesh, Assam, Manipur, Meghalaya, Mizoram, Nagaland, and Tripura —form a world unlike any other. Here, the air is purer, the forests are deeper, and the cultures are as diverse as the landscape itself. Whether you're chasing the monsoon in the wettest place on earth or seeking silence in ancient hilltop monasteries, this is where you come to find the parts of India that the maps often miss. Welcome to a land that stays with you long after you’ve left."
